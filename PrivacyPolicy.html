PILCPRO Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 6, 2026

PILCPRO ("the App") is a Pi-LC Butterworth high-pass filter design tool published by the PILCPRO developer. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App on your device.

1. Summary

  • The App is designed to work with data stored on your device.
  • We do not require account registration.
  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • Camera and photo library access are used only when you choose related features.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

The App may store the following locally on your iPhone or iPad:

  • Filter design history — parameters, computed component values, and timestamps saved in a local database.
  • Lab reference photos — images you attach in Design Workbench, saved in the App’s private documents area.
  • App preferences — settings needed for normal operation of the App.

This information remains on your device unless you delete it or remove the App.

3. Camera and Photo Library

The App may request permission to use:

  • Camera — to capture a reference photo for a filter design in Design Workbench.
  • Photo Library — to select an existing image as a reference photo.

These permissions are optional. If you deny access, you can still use filter design, history, comparison, and export features that do not require photos. Photos you add are kept locally on your device and are not uploaded by the App.

4. Share and Export

When you tap Share or Export, the App prepares a design report or image using content already on your device. You choose how and where to send that content through the iOS share sheet (for example, Mail, Messages, or Files). The App does not control what you do with exported content after you share it.

5. What We Do Not Collect

The App does not include:

  • User accounts or login systems
  • Advertising identifiers for targeted advertising
  • Contact lists, location tracking, or microphone recording
  • Automatic transmission of your design data to the developer

6. Data Retention and Deletion

You can manage your data as follows:

  • Clear saved design history from the History screen.
  • Remove reference photos in Design Workbench.
  • Delete all App data by uninstalling PILCPRO from your device.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for engineers, students, and hobbyists working with filter design. It is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will be revised when changes are made. Continued use of the App after an update means you accept the revised policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: PILCPRO36@icloud.com
