Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or PennyPath, please contact us at: support@pennypath.app

Welcome to PennyPath. We take your privacy seriously. PennyPath is a personal finance education app that offers lessons, quizzes, calculators, notes, and a glossary. Before using the app, please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By tapping "I Agree", you confirm that you have read and accepted this policy.

PennyPath is for educational purposes only and does not provide investment, tax, or legal advice.

2. Information We Collect

PennyPath works primarily on your device and does not require you to create an account. We may store the following information locally on your device:

Learning progress, including completed lessons, quiz scores, study streaks, and bookmarked glossary terms; notes you create, including titles, text, and optional photos; calculator history and result summaries; and app preferences, such as your privacy consent status.

When you use note features, the app may request access to your Photo Library or Camera. We only access photos you choose to attach. We do not collect payment information, government ID numbers, or precise location data.

3. How We Use Information

We use information stored on your device solely to provide and improve app features, including displaying your learning progress, saving notes and calculator results, recommending the next lesson, and helping you review quiz mistakes. We do not sell your personal information or use it for purposes unrelated to the app without your consent.

4. Storage and Protection

Most data is stored locally on your device using iOS storage mechanisms, including Application Support and UserDefaults. Notes and attached images are saved on your device until you delete them in the app or uninstall PennyPath.

We take reasonable measures to protect information on your device. However, no electronic storage method is completely secure. Please protect your device with a passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID.

5. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share information only when you have given explicit consent, when required by law or a competent authority, or when necessary to protect the legitimate rights and interests of you, us, or other users.

When you first launch the app, it may load this Privacy Policy over the internet. If you tap an external link (such as a mailto link), you may leave the app and be subject to the privacy practices of the destination service.

6. Your Rights

You may delete notes, calculation history, and quiz records within the app where available; manage Photo Library and Camera permissions in iOS Settings; and uninstall the app to remove locally stored data from your device. To ask questions or request assistance, please contact us using the information below.

7. Protection of Minors

PennyPath is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a minor, please read this policy with your guardian and use the app only with their consent. If you believe a child has provided information through the app, please contact us.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest version. Continued use of PennyPath after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
